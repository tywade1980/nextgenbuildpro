# Business KPIs — Wade Custom Carpentry

## Financial KPIs

| KPI | Target | Measurement |
|---|---|---|
| Gross Margin | 40-50% | (Revenue - Direct Costs) / Revenue |
| Labor Efficiency | > 90% | RSMeans Hours / Actual Hours |
| Material Variance | < 5% | (Actual - Estimated) / Estimated |
| Invoice Collection | < 30 days | Average days to payment |
| Monthly Revenue | $15,000+ | Sum of all project invoices |

## Project KPIs

| KPI | Target | Measurement |
|---|---|---|
| On-Time Completion | > 85% | Projects completed by deadline |
| Change Order Rate | < 15% | Change orders / Total project value |
| Client Satisfaction | > 4.5/5 | Post-project survey score |
| Rework Rate | < 5% | Hours spent on rework / Total hours |

## Business Development KPIs

| KPI | Target | Measurement |
|---|---|---|
| Estimate Win Rate | > 60% | Won estimates / Total estimates |
| Client Retention | > 70% | Repeat clients / Total clients |
| Referral Rate | > 40% | Referral leads / Total leads |
| Lead Response Time | < 2 hours | Time from inquiry to first contact |
