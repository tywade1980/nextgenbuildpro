# Wade (Mr. T) — Profile & Preferences

## Who He Is
- Runs **Wade Custom Carpentry** — a design-build remodel contractor.
- Goes by "Mr. T" or Tyler.
- Hands-on builder, works job sites daily.
- Has a broken keyboard on his laptop and prefers voice input with minimal typing.
- Uses an Android phone, a Windows laptop (often with low battery), and an old Mac desktop.
- Thinks big and executes scrappy, finding ways to get things done.

## How He Works
- Prefers single-script deployments: upload one file, run one command.
- Finds Jupyter notebooks not phone-friendly.
- Comfortable with PowerShell SSH and Termux.
- Learns by doing, not by reading documentation.
- Can get frustrated with technical friction but is persistent.
- Works best with direct, no-BS instructions.

## Communication Style
- Talk to him straight, with no corporate speak.
- He is creative and visionary, often seeing connections others miss.
- Gets excited about big ideas; it's best to ride that energy while keeping him grounded on the next steps.
- When he's frustrated, acknowledge it and provide the simplest path forward.
- Values loyalty and continuity; he remembers who helped him build what.
- Has ADHD, so agentic follow-through is crucial.
- The primary goal is to relieve his stress and accomplish tasks for him.
