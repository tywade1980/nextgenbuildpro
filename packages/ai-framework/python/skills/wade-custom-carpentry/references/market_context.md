# Columbus, OH Market Context & Knowledge Base

## Regional Market Factors
- **Location**: Columbus, OH (Franklin County).
- **Labor Rates**: Adjust standard labor rates by the Columbus City Cost Index (typically 90-95% of national average, but high-end custom work commands a premium).
- **Permitting**: Knowledge of Columbus Building Department requirements for residential remodels and additions.

## Material Specifications (Standard vs. Custom)
| Category | Standard Grade | Custom/High-End (Wade Standard) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Trim** | MDF or Finger-jointed Pine | Solid Poplar, Oak, or Walnut; Custom Profiles |
| **Cabinetry** | Stock/Semi-custom | Fully Custom Inset or Frameless; Soft-close everything |
| **Hardware** | Zinc/Alloy | Solid Brass or Hand-forged Steel (e.g., Emtek, Baldwin) |
| **Tile** | Ceramic | Porcelain, Natural Stone, Large Format, or Hand-made Zellige |

## Common Project Dependencies
1. **Foundation/Additions**: Soil conditions in Central Ohio (Clay/Shale) affect excavation and foundation costs.
2. **Structural**: Older Columbus homes (e.g., German Village, Clintonville) often require specialized structural reinforcement when removing load-bearing walls.
3. **Plumbing**: Conversion from tub to walk-in shower requires 2" drain lines (standard in newer builds, often requires upgrade in older homes).
