# Material Sourcing Guide for Wade Custom Carpentry

## Preferred Suppliers
- **Home Depot**: General construction materials, lumber, tools.
- **Lowe's**: General construction materials, appliances, lighting.
- **Ferguson**: Plumbing fixtures, HVAC, high-end kitchen and bath.
- **Floor & Decor**: Hard surface flooring, tile, stone, installation materials.
- **The Tile Shop**: Specialized tile, accents, design-forward ceramics.

## Search Strategy
1. **Identify Needs**: Extract material types and quantities from the Project Take-off.
2. **Check Grades**: Verify if "Contractor Grade", "Premium", or "Custom" is required.
3. **Price Comparison**:
   - Check availability at local branches (use zip code provided in project context).
   - Compare unit prices across at least two preferred suppliers for high-volume items.
4. **Link Collection**: Save direct URLs to product pages for client approval or purchase.

## Common Material Categories
- **Lumber**: Dimensional (2x4, 2x6), Plywood, OSB, Pressure Treated.
- **Plumbing**: Tubs (alcove vs free-standing), Shower valves, Faucets, Vanities.
- **Flooring**: Tile (Porcelain, Ceramic, Natural Stone), Underlayment, Grout.
- **Structural**: Foundation materials, concrete mix, rebar, framing hardware.
